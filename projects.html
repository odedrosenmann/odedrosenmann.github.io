StudyWay

A mobile and desktop website that provide tutoring services across a variety of academic subjects and across all age groups. We created a unique platform for both students and tutors to experience the most professional and interactive lesson they can get. We use the Internet to connect students to tutors for tutoring at anytime from anywhere, and help them gain confidence, develop study skills and improve grades.

Motivation

Identifying the trend of services conducted online. The Online Tutoring Services industry is in the growth stage of its economic life cycle. The industry is characterized by growth above the overall US economy which is projected to grow over the next 10-year period.

Things I learned

  • Created a mockup
  • Analyzed competitive landscape
  • Translated user needs to product specs

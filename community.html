StudyWay

A mobile and desktop website that provides tutoring services across a variety of academic subjects and across all age groups. We created a unique platform for both students and tutors to experience the most professional and interactive lesson they can get. We use the Internet to help students gain confidence, develop study skills and improve grades.

Motivation

Identifying the trend of services conducted online. The Online Tutoring Services industry is in the growth stage of its economic life cycle and is projected to grow over the next 10-year period.

Things I learned

  • Responsive web design concepts
  • Created a mockup
  • Analyzed competitive landscape
  • Translated user needs to product specs

CocktailMe

CocktailMe is a service that delivers an upscale cocktail bar experience through the seamless delivery of liquor, ingredients, tools, and glassware a customer needs to craft cocktails at home. The service also delivers mixology education through simple instructions that anyone can follow in order to make their preferred cocktail. CocktailMe enables the customer to impress his guests through a curated selection of cocktails at the convenience of home delivery.

Motivation

I have worked on this start up as part of New Venture Discovery class at Kellogg School of Management. My team and I identified a problem worth solving. Young professionals have grown tired of bars and prefer to spend time with friends at home, but they do not think they can deliver the same high quality craft cocktails at home. CocktailMe wants to solve this dissonance. The competitors in this market fail to provide an appropriate solution.

Things I learned

  • Translating a problem into viable product
  • Properly understand customers and their needs
  • Craft compeling value proposition
  • Develop well differentiated solution
  • Identify a repeatable and scalable solution
  • Mockups with POP App

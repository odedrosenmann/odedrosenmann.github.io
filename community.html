Paamonim

The Paamonim organization is a non-profit social organization that operates throughout Israel.
Paamonim provides assistance to individuals, families, groups and organizations, seeking economic education and counseling and interested in acquiring financial knowledge and tools, changing their consumer habits and taking economic responsibility.
The organization implements cutting-edge educational methodologies and new technologies, and encourages innovative, out-of-the-box ideas.

Financial Advisor at Paamonim

My community involvement included:

  • Provided financial guidance to families in economic distress
  • Set up debt management plans and built a secure financial future by shifting to a saving-oriented budget
  • Provided family business consulting services
